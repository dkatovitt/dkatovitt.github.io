Meet The Creator & CEO

Mr. Dipak Kumar Gupta

Founder, Creator, MD & CEO | From Kolkata

Mr. Dipak Kumar Gupta is a visionary entrepreneur and creative leader from Kolkata who established **DK Atovitt** with a singular mission: to redefine modern entertainment. Under his dynamic leadership, the production house has grown into a powerhouse encompassing full-scale movie production, commercial video direction, cutting-edge AI video generation, and comprehensive shooting services. His passion for meaningful cinema and technological innovation continues to drive the company to new heights.
